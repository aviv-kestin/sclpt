ABOUT SCLPT

SCLPT Systems is an electronics company producing standalone modular synths, eurorack modules, and other instruments designed to push your sonic practice further. With a focus on granular textures and chaotic modulation, our instruments thrive in allowing you to explore sounds yet to be discovered.
